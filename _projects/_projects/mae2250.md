## Client Pitch {#client-pitch}

The client pitch introduced a mechanical trapping system designed to reduce SLF contamination during vineyard harvest.

---

## Functional Prototype {#functional-prototype}

### System Overview
The prototype consists of a funnel entry system, container with drawer, and adjustable pole.

### Components
- Funnel: allows entry, prevents escape  
- Drawer: collects SLFs  
- Pole: adjustable height  

---

## Client Report {#client-report}

### Proposed Solution
A passive mechanical trap that captures SLFs using a funnel-flap system.

### How It Works
- SLFs enter through funnel  
- Flaps close behind them  
- Stored in removable drawer  

### Results
- Funnel prevents escape  
- Drawer holds required load  
- System works as integrated design  

### Next Steps
- Improve stability  
- Add weather protection  
- Refine funnel geometry
